# mixmi Alpha Walkthrough Video Scripts

Video guides to help alpha users understand the platform, upload content, and start creating.

**Audience:** Alpha users (creators, musicians, DJs, visual artists)
**Tone:** Friendly, concise, no time wasting, co-building vibes
**Length range:** 30 seconds to 2 minutes

---
